Map Page

This page displays the map. The map can be zoomed in and out with the buttons in the top left corner. The bottom right button locates your GPS and positions the map in the centre.

When diary entries are entered, they will appear as pins on the map and when clicked, the date and GPS coordinates will be displayed. You can choose to edit and share this survey. To create a diary entry from the map, click the top right “+” button which will display the diary entry survey.

Diary Page

This page displays all diary entries made in a scroll screen. You can edit an entry by clicking Edit Entry under the map icon and date.

The “+” button in the centre bottom of the screen above the tabs, can be clicked to start a new diary entry.

Diary Entry Survey

This page pops up when prompted by buttons in the Map (“+” button) or in the diary page (bottom centre above tabs “+” button).

Fill in the first question:

  • Select from the three options provided.

  • If last option click coordinates to select your location.

  • Tap the pin and a red cross and green tick should appear with a blue pin on the map.

  • By zooming in and out and moving the map, click on the map where you want your pin to be placed.

  • You can add layers of information to the map (SOURCE: GEOSCIENCE AUSTRALIA), to help you find your location, by clicking on the map layers icon.

  • Click green tick when your happy with your pin location, or red to hide pin.

  • Check coordinates on survey, if incorrect, select coordinates again.

  • Click next.

Fill in the remaining questions by selecting from the options available or moving the circle along the line to your desired rating.

Repeat for the night location.

Click Submit to upload your completed survey or swipe left to view answers.

At any time during the survey you can swipe left to view previous answers.

After completing the survey a Pin should appear on your map and when clicked, the date and gps coordinates will be displayed. You can choose to edit and share survey.

To finish the diary

Continue making daily diary entries until you depart the Kimberley (or return home if you are a Kimberley resident). You can finalise the survey by clicking on the “Finalise Survey” button found at the top of the Diary tab. To successfully finalise the survey you will need mobile reception or WiFi. After you click this button you can then uninstall KimTrip from your smart phone.
